What happens next?

  1. we'll ask you a few questions about your product or the work you do, this allows us to matchmake you with the right researchers and participants
  2. when you are ready we'll send you the link to write out a research brief - so we do the most impactful research we can
  3. you'll have the option to pay for that specific bit of research or buy research in bulk to save some extra £££
  4. Then the magic happens... we'll recommend you researchers and once you pick the one that suits your needs off we go!
