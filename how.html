Making research simple

How does this work?

We've made the process super simple for you. Essentially we connect you to the people you need and do all the leg work so you get the insights to build better products.

What happens?

  1. You SIGN UP!
  2. You'll submit a research brief
  3. We'll matchmake you with a skilled researcher
  4. You'll pick the researcher best for you
  5. We'll work with you to make sure we are asking the right questions to get incredible insights
  6. We'll organise research sessions at a convienient time for you and send you all the links to join in
  7. We'll matchmake you with a skilled researcher
  8. We'll send you a clear and summarised list of key insights
  9. We'll also organise a show and tell so the research is communicated with the people that need to hear the findings
